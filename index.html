Meet the Team

Dawnie L. Kildoo, DMD, MAGD

While known for her caring touch and with over twenty-six years experience, Dr. Dawnie Kildoo believes in helping people to keep their teeth for a lifetime—no matter what their age! Keeping current on the latest advanced dental technology, she and her team practice complete dentistry, educating patients on the connection between dental health and overall health.

A graduate of Georgetown University Dental School, Dr. Kildoo was awarded the prestigious Master of Academy of General Dentistry (MAGD) from the Academy of General Dentistry (AGD). This award is given to only one percent of the 35,000 dentists in the AGD and is a result of over 1100 credit hours in continuing education in seventeen different disciplines.

Christopher Chin, DMD, MPH

Dr. Chin believes in personalized, comprehensive dentistry. Your mouth is the gateway to your body and should be treated as an important part of your overall well-being. He strives to give his patients the tools and techniques to improve the quality of life for his patients through having a healthy mouth.

Dr. Chin received his dental degree from the Arizona School of Dentistry and Oral Health, and later attended A.T. Still University’s School of Health Management with a Master of Public Health with a dental emphasis to enhance his ability to serve underserved communities.

Dr. Kildoo

Dr. Kildoo is married and has four children, four grandchildren, one Jack Russell terrier dog, and a quarter horse named Cayenne. She loves to hike, ride, and spend time with her family.

Dr. Kildoo is a member of the American Dental Association, the Academy of General Dentistry, the Pima County Dental Society, the Arizona Dental Association, the American Academy of Sleep Medicine, the American Academy of Dental Sleep Medicine, and the American Academy of Cosmetic Dentistry. Dr. Kildoo is also an alumnus of the prestigious Pankey Institute.

Dr. Chin

Throughout his career he has been involved in numerous community outreach projects. This includes the ADA sponsored Give Kids a Smile and the nationally recognized UCSD Pre-Dental Society free dental clinics. He finds joy in giving back to the communities wherever possible.

Dr. Chin is from San Diego, California where his father is a practicing dentist and his mother is a nurse. Growing up in a medical family he was instilled with the values and responsibilities of the dental profession from a young age. He is married to a pediatric dentist who shares his love for dentistry. They have a dog named Bullet and enjoy many outdoor activities like biking, running and hiking. During the winter he and his wife enjoy visiting colder portions of the world to ski and snowboard.

Dr. Chin serves as adjunct faculty with Arizona School of Dentistry and Oral Health. The focus on teaching underscores his commitment to continued education and refinement of his skills and knowledge. To this end, he is also a member of the Academy of General Dentistry (AGD) and the American Dental Association (ADA) where the culture of life-long learning is fostered.

Our Services

Dental Implants

Dental implants are a great option for replacing missing teeth, improving chewing ability, eliminating bridges, and replacing dentures.

Several implants may provide a base for a series of artificial teeth known as a fixed bridge. Implants can even be used to secure a full set of removable dentures for people who have no remaining natural teeth.

LEARN MORE

Cosmetic Dentistry

Cosmetic dentistry is one of the most cost-effective ways to improve your appearance. A beautiful, bright, healthy smile will increase self-confidence and overall life satisfaction. Cosmetic dentistry includes things like tooth-colored restorations, dental implants, veneers, and crowns. We have many options, so please call us today!
